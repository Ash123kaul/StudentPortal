Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password". Enter your registered email address and follow the instructions sent to your email.

How can I view my course schedule?

Your course schedule is available in the Dashboard section. You can also find it under the "Courses" tab where you can see your timetable for the current semester.

Where can I find my grades?

Grades are available in the "Courses" section. Click on the specific course to view your assignment scores, exam results, and overall grade for the course.

How do I make a payment for tuition?

Tuition payments can be made through the "Payment" section. You can pay using credit/debit cards, bank transfer, or other available payment methods.

What should I do if I can't access my account?

If you're having trouble accessing your account, first try resetting your password. If that doesn't work, contact the IT support team using the contact information below.
